# TC02 - Koel | Artists | User is able to play a song of a selected artist

## Preconditions
- User is registered and logged into Koel: https://qa.koel.app/#!/home  
- User is on the Artists page of the Koel app: https://qa.koel.app/#!/artists  
- Artists and their songs exist in the database

## Test Data
- Selected artist: Enter Value...  
- Selected song: Enter Value...

## Steps
- Click on a specific artist  
- Observe the list of songs displayed for that artist  
- Click on a song by the selected artist  
- Click the play button  
- Observe the playback behaviour

## Expected Result
- The user is taken to a page displaying songs of the selected artist.  
- The selected song begins playing successfully.

## Actual Result
- The user was taken to the artist’s song list page.  
- The selected song by the selected artist started playing successfully.
